HOW TO PLAY

Never played Salad Bowl before?
Don’t worry we’ve got you covered

Grab your friends and put those kitchen bowls away

Welcome to our modern take on an a popular classic. This digital game allows you to spend more time enjoying your wacky friends and less time searching for a pen and paper.

Once everyone adds words to the deck you may begin

Pick a host and have them set up your new favorite group activity. Once everyone has joined a team and added their hardest word choices to the pile the game can begin on the host's phone.

Each round your team will guess as many words possible.

Work together with the rest of your team to interpret what your question master is trying to say to earn the most points. There are no passes in this game so don’t give up early!

If your team gets the most cards correct you win!

The player with the most points at the end of the game with be crowned victorious! Use our final breakdown to read a brief summary about each word.
